# PixelPitch - Platform Specs Reference

Pixel dimensions, aspect ratios, safe zones, and composition guidance per platform.

---

## LINKEDIN (Primary Platform)

### Post Image - Landscape
- Dimensions: 1200 x 627 px
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Safe zone: Keep key content within center 80%
- Tip: Place subject slightly left-of-center, right edge may crop on mobile

### Post Image - Square (Best for feed visibility)
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1080 px
- Aspect ratio: 1:1
- Safe zone: Center 80% square
- Tip: Works perfectly on both desktop and mobile

### Post Image - Portrait (Highest stop-scroll potential)
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1350 px
- Aspect ratio: 4:5
- Safe zone: Center 85% to avoid corner cropping in grid view
- Tip: Leave top 20% for headline text overlay

### LinkedIn Article / Newsletter Cover
- Dimensions: 1200 x 644 px
- Aspect ratio: ~1.87:1
- Tip: Leave center 60% for main visual, title will overlay top-left area

### LinkedIn Profile Banner
- Dimensions: 1584 x 396 px
- Aspect ratio: 4:1
- Tip: Profile photo overlaps bottom-left ~200x200px - keep that corner clear

### LinkedIn Carousel (Document Post - PDF slides)
- Per slide: 1080 x 1080 px (square) or 1080 x 1350 px (portrait)
- Slides: 5-10 is the sweet spot for engagement
- Tip: Slide 1 is the cover - make it a standalone hero image
- Tip: Keep consistent visual margins (60px) across all slides

### LinkedIn Story
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1920 px
- Aspect ratio: 9:16

## INSTAGRAM

### Square Post
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1080 px
- Aspect ratio: 1:1

### Portrait Post (Highest feed visibility)
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1350 px
- Aspect ratio: 4:5
- Safe zone: Center 85% to avoid corner cropping in grid view

### Landscape Post
- Dimensions: 1080 x 566 px
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Note: Smaller in feed - less recommended for primary content

### Stories / Reels Cover
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1920 px
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Safe zone: Keep key content within center 70% (avoid top 15% and bottom 20% - UI overlays)

### Instagram Carousel
- Per slide: 1080 x 1080 px
- Up to 10 slides. First image acts as cover.

---

## TWITTER / X

### In-Feed Image
- Recommended: 1600 x 900 px
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Tip: Feed preview crops top and bottom. Place important content in CENTER horizontal band (avoid top 20% and bottom 20%)

### Profile Header / Banner
- Dimensions: 1500 x 500 px
- Aspect ratio: 3:1
- Note: Profile photo overlaps bottom-left - keep clear

---

## FACEBOOK

### Post Image
- Recommended: 1200 x 630 px
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Also works: 1080 x 1080 px (square)

### Page Cover
- Recommended upload: 1640 x 624 px
- Safe zone (all devices): Center 820 x 312 px area

### Facebook Story
- Dimensions: 1080 x 1920 px
- Safe zone: Center 60% to avoid UI overlays

---

## BLOG / WEBSITE

### Hero / Featured Image
- Recommended: 1200 x 628 px or 1920 x 1080 px
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 1.91:1
- Tip: If title overlays the image, keep the right 40% clear

### Open Graph / Social Preview
- Recommended: 1200 x 630 px
- This appears when your URL is shared on LinkedIn, Twitter, Slack, etc.

## YOUTUBE

### Thumbnail
- Recommended: 1280 x 720 px
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Tip: Thumbnails viewed very small in search results - use bold text, high contrast, clear focal point
- Safe zone: Center 85%

### Channel Banner
- Recommended: 2560 x 1440 px
- Safe zone (all devices): Center 1546 x 423 px only
